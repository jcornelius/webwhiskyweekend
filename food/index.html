The Food

Your Web Whisky Weekend ticket includes all the food and drink for the entire weekend (unless we decide to go out for a meal, but who would do that?). All food is prepared fresh on site by people who really know how to cook.

Previous year's menus have included:

Breakfast

  • Fresh scrambled migas with bell pepper, jalapeño, onion, and chorizo
  • Breakfast omelettes with spinach, sausage or bacon, with white cheddar cheese grits
  • Steel-cut oatmeal
  • Fresh fruit

Lunch/Dinner

  • Angus Taco salad with roasted red peppers, yellow hominy, and fresh guacamole
  • Homemade veal and sausage bolognese over fresh pasta with romaine salad
  • Pulled pork tacos smoked on-site for 12 hours with fresh slaw and guacamole
  • Fresh-trimmed steak tenderloins seared in cast iron and finished in 600° oven
  • Broiled salmon with garlic and rosemary
  • BBQ side of salmon over 3-cheese grits
  • Sides include:
    • French-cut green beans,
    • sautéed balsamic mushrooms,
    • sweet potato mash,
    • Bibb lettuce salad with crushed pistachios and stilton cheese
    • Carrot and vegetable medley
    • Grilled zucchini and summer squash
    • Cucumber, tomato, and onion salad
  • Homemade bourbon apple pie and ice cream

All the snacks

There is an assortment of fruits, chips, dips, and other snacks including a selection of charcuterie and gourmet cheeses.
