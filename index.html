WEB WHISKY WEEKEND 6

A weekend getaway for people who enjoy fine whisky, amazing food, and great conversation.

LAKE LANIER, GA // OCTOBER 8-11, 2020

For the 6th year in a row - designers, developers, entrepreneurs, and investors come to a resort style lake estate for a weekend of relaxation in the company of smart people.

Everyone knows the best part of any conference is seeing friends you normally only see online, meeting new people, and all the conversations that happen over food and drink.

Web Whisky Weekend is just that part. No badges. No sessions. No bullshit. Just a group of like-minded people getting together to talk about the Web and enjoy some tasty brown liquids.

Imagine a resort style villa on a lake in the North Georgia mountains for a weekend to hang out, talk about the web, and enjoy some good whisky. Everyone brings a bottle or two (or more) of something to share. There are typically over 100 unique bottles to sample and savor.

It's one helluva weekend.

Epic Food and Drinks

Over 100 Unique Bottles of Whisky

You read that correctly. We have a better whisky selection than most bars and restaurants. So many, in fact, that we had to build an app to track our tasting notes. From limited release Kentucky bourbon to rare small-batch Scotch, there's something for every palette and to go with every meal.

We also stock a supply of wine, beer, spirits, and mixers. You'll find something tasty. Guaranteed.

The food doesn't suck, either.

Your reservation includes breakfast, lunch, and dinner on all three nights. We'll have options for carnivores and vegetarians alike. It's all family style and better than most restaurants.

Expect steak and salmon dinners and healthy hearty breakfasts prepared by a former chef. Special diets will be accommodated. We can also hit up a local grocer to stock up on goodies for the weekend so you can pick up whatever what you want to have handy.

Travel and Transportation

The resort is on Lake Lanier, about 45 minutes north-east of downtown Atlanta.Live near Atlanta? Sweet. Drive on up, there's plenty of parking. It's an easy drive from just about everywhere in the metro-area. We'll send directions via email once you reserve your spot.

Coming from out of town?

You can fly into Hartsfield-Jackson International (ATL). We will help arrange transport from the airport to the house and back again. Just let us know your itinerary after you register.
