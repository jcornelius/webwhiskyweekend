WEB WHISKY WEEKEND 5

A weekend getaway for people who enjoy fine whisky, amazing food, and great conversation.

LAKE LANIER, GA // OCTOBER 3rd-6th, 2019

Designers, developers, entrepreneurs, and investors come to a resort style lake estate for a weekend of relaxation in the company of smart people.

Everyone knows the best part of any conference is seeing friends you normally only see online, meeting new people, and all the conversations that happen over food and drink.

Web Whisky Weekend is just that part. No badges. No sessions. No bullshit. Just a group of like-minded people getting together to talk about the Web and enjoy some tasty brown liquids.

Imagine a resort style villa on a lake in the North Georgia mountains for a weekend to hang out, talk about the web, and enjoy some good whisky. Everyone brings a bottle or two (or more) of something to share. There are typically over 100 unique bottles to sample and savor.

It's one helluva weekend.
